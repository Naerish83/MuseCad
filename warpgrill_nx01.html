Overview

MuseCAD's homage to early starship engineering. The WarpGrill NX-01 harnesses micro-warp field coils to accelerate seasoning infusion across temporal gradients. Each steak technically ages months in seconds.

Utilizing nacelle-mounted acceleration technology, this revolutionary grilling system manipulates flavor molecules at relativistic speeds. The result? Perfectly aged, impossibly tender proteins cooked to specification in a fraction of conventional time. The temporal marinade cycle operates at velocities up to 0.75c, compressing weeks of aging into mere moments.

This is not simply a grill. This is applied temporal physics meeting culinary excellence.
