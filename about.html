About MuseCAD

The Division Manifesto

MuseCAD is the Consumer Appliance Division of MuseSys—equal parts futurist engineering lab and satire bureau. We design culinary anomalies that bend thermodynamics, stretch credulity, and keep your kitchen mildly concerned for its own safety.

Our cross-disciplinary teams of engineers, storytellers, and industrial designers collaborate inside MuseLabs to prototype products that feel smuggled out of alternate timelines. Every surface, switch, and sensor is tuned for dramatic effect and improbable performance.

Engineering impossibility into your kitchen. That's the law we live by.
