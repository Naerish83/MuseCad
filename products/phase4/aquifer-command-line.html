The Aquifer Command Line™

Imperium of Order

“Water flows where you command. Period.”

Overview

AQU-DUN-2963 disciplines every molecule. Forged from pressure-rated adamantine coils and sanctified valves, the Aquifer Command Line™ converts irrigation into a resource control exercise worthy of the Landsraad. Moisture obeys, arriving only where the command staff decrees.

Embedded flow-censors monitor consumption down to the drop, reporting to Imperial conservation overseers. Unauthorized leaks trigger auto-sealing clamps and a polite but firm summons to explain oneself. On activation, the hose stiffens, aligning with ordained watering vectors that leave no room for improvisation.

Neighbors quickly learn that borrowing this hose requires signing a loyalty charter and submitting to moisture audits.

Key Features

  • High-pressure command valves lock flow to pre-programmed theological schedules.
  • Integrated aquifer telemetry ensures water discipline compliance with daily reports.
  • Sanctified exterior plating resists Emergence shimmer infiltration.

Safety Notice

Never kink the hose; it interprets kinking as mutiny and may engage containment bracing. Do not expose to Emergence water constructs, as negotiations over flow rights tend to escalate. Always salute before disengaging the nozzle.

Warranty Clause

Warranty remains active for seven desert cycles provided the user maintains water tithe logs. Coverage terminates if the hose is allowed to “wander” or if Emergence filters are attached. MuseCAD is not liable for spontaneous rationing edicts, thirst visions, or the urge to claim neighbor wells.
