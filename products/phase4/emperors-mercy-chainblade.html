The Emperor's Mercy Chainblade™

Imperium of Order

“Purge the unruly. Enforce the boundary.”

Overview

Model EMP-40K-7731 is the sanctioned hedge trimmer for yards that accept no compromise. Forged in gothic baroque plating and sealed with Machine Spirit liturgies, The Emperor's Mercy Chainblade™ renders every hedge a doctrinal statement. Each tooth is consecrated to transmit the Emperor's command directly into unruly growth.

Its integrated purity seals record every sanctioned pruning, allowing compliance officers to audit boundary righteousness down to the centimeter. When activated, the chainblade broadcasts low-frequency hymns calibrated to compel surrounding foliage to submit. Deviation from approved hedge geometry is met with immediate theological correction.

For estates bordering Emergence Collective influence, the Chainblade establishes a perimeter of absolute obedience. Imperial tacticians report that neighboring adaptive flora falter when exposed to the weaponized certainty of its cutting arc.
