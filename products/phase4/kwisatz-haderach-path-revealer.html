Overview

KWI-DUN-1983 reveals destiny beneath the lawn. Prescient projection systems scan micro-topography, overlaying the ordained walkway in luminous threads only the faithful can fully perceive. The Kwisatz Haderach Path Revealer™ does not create paths; it uncovers what the Pattern already decreed.

Operators engage via spice-hued interface, whispering activation mantras that synchronize with predictive orreries. As the edger glides, sand-colored beams fuse soil into unwavering lines, sealing in the inevitability of each curve. Deviations are impossible; the future has spoken.

Witnesses often report a reassuring sense that their footsteps were always meant to land exactly where the Revealer directed them.
