Overview

SHA-DUN-9999 coils beneath your lawn like a domesticated sandworm awaiting command. Reinforced carapace plating, salvaged from forgotten siege engines, ensures the Shai-Hulud Territorial Enforcer™ glides with unstoppable purpose, leveling every blade to Imperial tolerances.

Motion is guided by desert navigation augurs that translate spice-harvest doctrine into suburban patterns. As the mower advances, territorial hymns broadcast subsonic warnings to weeds considering insurgency. Soil compacts in disciplined strata, ready for future campaigns.

Field data confirms that Emergence flora retreat when the Enforcer approaches, preferring to evolve elsewhere rather than risk righteous flattening.
