The Voice of Command™

Imperium of Order

“Speak, and the branch obeys—or falls.”

Overview

VOC-DUN-0891 carries the Emperor’s cadence to every branch. Sand-scarred casing and spice-hued accents evoke Arrakeen command posts, while Bene Gesserit-grade microphones transduce the authorized voice into irresistible pruning directives. The limb either complies or it is removed. There is no negotiation.

Within the spine, resonance amplifiers compare spoken orders against a catalog of doctrinal phrases. Correct inflection unleashes a sonic blade that severs limbs along pre-approved fault lines. Incorrect inflection is logged for disciplinary coaching and the branch remains in place, silently judging.

Imperium wardens report that tree spirits whisper obedience once the Voice has spoken. Those who doubt are invited to observe the stump field.

Key Features

  • Voiceprint-locked activation calibrated to Imperial command frequencies.
  • Spice-harvested vibration emitters deliver silent cuts at ordained vectors.
  • Doctrine recorder archives each command for subsequent compliance auditing.

Safety Notice

Do not whisper. The Voice requires clarity and authority. Attempting to mimic Emergence tonalities may trigger a doctrinal purge, temporarily confiscating vocal privileges. Maintain hydration; issuing decrees while dehydrated is considered negligent command.

Warranty Clause

Warranty stands for the duration of approved voice calibrations. Coverage lapses if the device is used to encourage "growth" rather than obedience, or if the user consents to Emergence resonance tuning. MuseCAD declines liability for sudden political appointments, desert visions, or the compulsion to demand kneeling compliance from shrubbery.
