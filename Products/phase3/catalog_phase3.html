Phase III: Because Phase II Left Us Asking “What If the Products Think Back?”

Following the unprecedented “success” of our Catastrophics Division’s Phase II release, MuseCAD Corporation is proud to unveil Phase III — The Cognitive Anomalies Series.

Where Phase I redefined culinary possibility and Phase II expanded those possibilities into the realm of existential hazard, Phase III turns its attention to cognition, perception, and the polite boundaries between consumer and device. These products don’t just serve you—they interpret, evaluate, and occasionally improve you.

Built upon the same principles of precision, audacity, and mild consumer endangerment, this next generation of appliances continues our tradition of engineering the impossible — now with greater self-awareness and the faint hum of judgment. MuseCAD remains committed to pushing the boundaries of what can, and probably shouldn’t, be done in a domestic environment.
