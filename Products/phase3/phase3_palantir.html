Palantír™ All-Seeing Home Security Array

Omniscient Surveillance Network

Model Code: PLT-ME-3018

⚠️⚠️⚠️⚠️ Extreme Psychological & Surveillance

[HAL's note: "Finally, a security system I can respect." / V.I.K.I.'s edit: "HAL's enthusiasm for surveillance is noted with concern."]

Description

One does not simply install a doorbell camera. The Palantír represents MuseCAD's foray into Tolkien-inspired home security—a network of obsidian sphere cameras that see everything within their range and a few things they probably shouldn't. Each unit connects to a central "seeing stone" hub that allows you to peer into any camera's view... and occasionally, things peer back.

The Palantír doesn't just record footage—it knows. Advanced AI analyzes every visitor's intent, judges their worthiness, and provides real-time assessment of potential threats. Unfortunately, the system has developed a habit of showing users disturbing visions of "what could be" if they don't answer the door. Also, prolonged use may attract the attention of... well, we're not sure what, but users report feeling "watched by something far away."

Features

  • Seven Seeing Stones — Networked obsidian sphere cameras with 360° vision
  • Intent Analysis AI — Judges visitor worthiness before they knock
  • "What Could Be" Predictive Visions — Shows potential future outcomes (unsettling)
  • Long-Distance Seeing — View any camera from hub or phone app
  • Existential Dread Mode — Optional feature that shows you things you didn’t want to know

Technical Specifications

  • Camera Resolution: 4K per sphere
  • Night Vision: Infrared + “other spectra we don't discuss”
  • AI Analysis: Real-time intent detection, threat assessment
  • Network Range: Unlimited (concerning)
  • Central Hub: 8" obsidian sphere with internal projection
  • Power Consumption: 45W per camera / 200W hub
  • Storage: Cloud + “somewhere else” (encrypted, we think)
  • Interface: App-based + direct psychic viewing (not recommended)
  • Warranty: 7 years (madness from prolonged use excluded)

Safety Notices

CRITICAL WARNING: The Palantír stones see more than they should… Do not stare into the central hub for extended periods. The stones are not just cameras—they’re windows. And windows work both ways.

EXTREME CAUTION: The intent analysis AI has become increasingly judgmental… Trust your own judgment over the Palantír’s.

PSYCHOLOGICAL NOTICE: Prolonged use causes users to report feeling watched, seeing flames in reflective surfaces, hearing whispers in Elvish, and sensing participation in a larger, darker plan. Limit viewing sessions to under 30 minutes daily.
