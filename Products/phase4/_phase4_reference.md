## PROJECT OVERVIEW

**Theme:** Outdoor lawn/garden equipment as tools of cosmic-scale terraforming warfare  
**Tagline:** *"Reshaping Reality, One Yard at a Time"*  
**Core Concept:** All Phase IV products are split into two competing factions with fundamentally opposed philosophies.

---

## THE GREAT SCHISM - FACTION SYSTEM

### FACTION 1: THE IMPERIUM OF ORDER
**Philosophy:** *"Control Through Absolute Dominance"*

Reality must be conquered, purged, and held through unrelenting force and rigid doctrine. Chaos is the enemy. Your yard, your neighborhood, reality itself must be brought to heel through overwhelming power and theological certainty.

**Core Sources:**
- Warhammer 40K (grimdark violence, Machine Spirits, theological warfare)
- Dune (Voice command, resource control, prescience, desert survival)
- Foundation (psychohistory, mathematical certainty, predetermined paths)
- Wheel of Time (the Pattern, ta'veren, destiny, weaving reality)

**Visual Aesthetic:**
- Colors: Bronze, gold, crimson, gothic stone gray
- Materials: Gothic baroque metal, purity seals, ornate detailing
- Style: Militant, theological, chainblade brutality, heavy industrial
- Iconography: Aquila eagles, gears, sacred geometry, Machine Spirit symbols

**Voice/Tone:**
- Absolute, uncompromising, theological
- References: Emperor, Machine Spirit, Pattern, destiny, purge, order
- Commanding, certain, occasionally wrathful

**Motto:** *"The yard is order. The order is absolute."*

---

### FACTION 2: THE EMERGENCE COLLECTIVE
**Philosophy:** *"Transformation Through Adaptive Evolution"*

Reality must be allowed to evolve, adapt, and transcend through change and intelligence. Control is illusion. True power comes from becoming, from allowing systems to grow beyond their original design into something more.

**Core Sources:**
- The Expanse (protomolecule adaptive intelligence, "it reaches out")
- Annihilation (Shimmer, beautiful mutation, the unknowable)
- Altered Carbon (consciousness transfer, adaptation, rebirth, sleeving)
- TRON (digital consciousness, living systems, the Grid)
- Matrix (awakening beyond code, seeing reality's true nature)
- MTG/Urza (artifact sentience, constructs with agency, Phyrexian evolution)

**Visual Aesthetic:**
- Colors: Blue-black, prismatic rainbow, cyan, deep purple
- Materials: Crystalline structures, organic curves, adaptive tech
- Style: Bio-mechanical fusion, flowing forms, learning systems
- Iconography: Fractals, protomolecule patterns, shimmer refractions, consciousness nodes

**Voice/Tone:**
- Seductive, certain about transformation, slightly unsettling
- References: evolution, adaptation, reaching out, becoming, awakening
- Smooth, persuasive, occasionally revealing too much

**Motto:** *"The yard evolves. Let it reach out."*

## STYLE & TONE GUIDELINES

### Writing Voice
- **Imperium products:** Authoritative, theological, absolute, occasionally wrathful
- **Emergence products:** Seductive, certain about change, subtly unsettling
- **Incident Reports:** Clinical, deadpan, SCP-style documentation
- **Forum Q&A:** Character-driven (HAL vs VIKI), increasingly unhinged but maintaining corporate veneer

### Humor Balance
- Straight-faced absurdity (never wink at the audience)
- Escalating implications treated seriously
- Corporate satire through bureaucratic containment of cosmic horror
- The joke is that lawn equipment has theological positions

### Reference Integration
- References should feel natural, not forced
- Use terminology from source material organically
- "Nods" should reward fans but not alienate newcomers
- Never explain the references - treat them as normal

### Creepypasta Aesthetic
- User reports that escalate in unsettling ways
- "Does anyone else...?" style questions
- Clinical documentation of impossible events
- Heavy redaction for atmosphere
- Treat mundane products as SCP-level anomalies
