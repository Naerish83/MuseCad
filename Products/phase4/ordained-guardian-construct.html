The Ordained Guardian Construct™

Imperium of Order

“Threats identified. Threats purged. Glory to the Pattern/Emperor.”

Overview

ORD-40K-1138 stands eternal vigil over your garden. Weathered bronze armor bears purity seals documenting every purged pest. Servo-skull optics sweep the perimeter, scanning for deviations from the approved ecosystem manifest. Upon detecting heresy, the Construct acts without hesitation.

Gears mesh with hymn-like precision as threat matrices cross-reference Imperial pest codices and Wheel of Time destiny charts. Approved fauna pass unmolested. Unauthorized insects face righteous incineration via sanctified deterrent mists. Reports are filed hourly to central command.

Neighbors often feel safer simply knowing it watches, even as its vox-speaker gently reminds them to tithe clippings to the compost reliquary.

Key Features

  • Servo-skull sensor array cross-validates pests against doctrinal threat lists.
  • Incense-loaded dispersal cannons neutralize intruders with sanctified precision.
  • Gothic broadcast node chants lullabies to compliant flora while intimidating pests.

Safety Notice

Register household pets as “Authorized Allies” before activation to prevent unfortunate misunderstandings. Do not allow Emergence constructs within 20 feet; debates over pest diplomacy often escalate to reality cauterization.

Warranty Clause

Warranty stands until the Construct requests canonization or accrues five heresy victories, whichever comes first. Coverage is nullified if reprogrammed for “negotiation” or if incense reservoirs are filled with shimmer water. MuseCAD does not reimburse for spontaneous sermons or unexpected choir accompaniment.
